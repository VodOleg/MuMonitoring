Mu Monitor service, allows you to monitor your muonline instances. The monitor will send you mail when your mu online process disconnected, closed or the character died and it is resting in safe zone. Very simple to use, no register required. Only download, launch the app log to your web session with the session name and key and submit your email for notifications. The monitor does not interact with the mu process and does not require you any credentials, it queries Windows for information regaring mu process. Safe, Simple and helpfull. Enjoy!
